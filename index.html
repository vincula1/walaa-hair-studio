Meet

With over 10 years of experience and a unique background in social psychology, Walaa brings both heart and expertise to every appointment.

Originally trained as a social worker, Walaa discovered her true calling after moving to the United States. She realized that helping women feel confident through beautiful hair was another powerful way to make a positive impact. This revelation led her to pursue extensive training in hairstyling, taking classes across the USA and internationally to perfect her craft.

Her dedication to continuous learning means she's always bringing fresh techniques and the latest trends to her clients. But what truly sets Walaa apart is her understanding of people—she doesn't just style hair, she creates an experience where every client feels heard, cared for, and leaves feeling their absolute best.
