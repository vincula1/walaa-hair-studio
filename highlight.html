Partial Highlights/Lowlights

$280+

Partial highlights and lowlights offer the perfect solution for adding dimension and interest to your hair without the commitment of a full head application. At Walaa Hair Studio, this service focuses on strategic placement around the face, crown, and top layers where color has the most impact. Whether you're looking to brighten your complexion with face-framing highlights or add depth with rich lowlights, Walaa expertly weaves color to create natural-looking dimension. This technique is ideal for enhancing your natural color, maintaining previous highlights, or creating subtle seasonal changes. The result is beautifully blended color that adds movement and vibrancy to your style while keeping maintenance manageable.
